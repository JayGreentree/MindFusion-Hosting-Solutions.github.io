Generosity

We believe that having a kickass church infrastructure shouldn't have to cost a pound of flesh.

In fact we think it should be as cheap as you can afford. That's why all of our plugins are available for nothing, as free as the air you breathe, on Github as well as for purchase on the Rock Store. In fact generosity is such a driving fact in the way we do business we give 50% of our net-income to the Spark Development Network to fund the development of Rock. Oh and our rates are pretty good too.

See How Much it Costs for Consultancy

Open Source

Did we mention all of our projects are on Github?

We believe in open source, it's one of the reasons we love Rock so much. All of our plugins are licensed under the Apache 2.0 license meaning you are free to peek under the hood at the code, alter it, and even distribute it yourself. Open source means you can rest easy knowing you can't ever be held to ransom by closed code.

What We Do

Custom Development

We produce plugins for sale in the Rock Store ranging from Slack integration to custom communication components. In addition, if you've got a need that isn't quite met by Rock or any existing plugins on the market we may just be the droids help you're looking for.

Consultancy

Figuring out where to start with Rock can be hard. We've been there since pre-1.0 and we gotten several Rock t-shirts along the way. Whether it be a conversation about whether Rock is right for you, initial setup, project planning, post-setup tweaks, advanced configuration, or any combination of buzzwords you can think of we'd like to see if we can help.
