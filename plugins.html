Our Take on Free

feat. 'The Free Promise'

Not all churches have an IT budget. If you're a lone volunteer at a church using Rock or belong to a church that's struggling to cover the rent this month - we get it. That's why all of our plugins are available for free on Github. What's free now will be free tomorrow so there's no fear that we'll update a plugin and you'll be stuck on an older version or that a plugin will suddenly disappear.

Three Compelling Reasons to Buy

You

Purchasing on the Rock Store takes aways the trouble of installing and keeping your plugin up to date. Why bother with FTP when you can automatically keep in sync with the updates as they come?

Us

Bricks and Mortar is a one man endeavour. Purchasing plugins on the Rock Store helps pay for things like my rent, test servers, and for me to not have a job so I can contribute to Rock in the time I'm not studying.

The Community

50% of all of Bricks and Mortar revenue goes to the Spark Development Network to fund the continual development of Rock, by purchasing you're directly supporting the folks who make Rock a reality.
